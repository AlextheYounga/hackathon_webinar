# [Replit Agent](https://replit.com)

[Replit Agent](https://replit.com) is useful because Replit combines the editor, runtime, terminal, hosting, and AI assistant in the browser.

## Good For

* Students who do not want local setup
* Simple web apps
* Class demos
* Projects that need quick deployment
* Teams that want to share a live URL

## Watch Out For

* Hosted environments have limits
* Some packages may behave differently than on your own computer
* You still need to understand how to run and debug the app
